! Simplified Ad Blocker - Blocks Ads & Trackers Without Breaking Websites
! This filter is designed to block intrusive ads, trackers, and auto-opening tabs, ensuring functionality on mobile Chromium browsers.

! Block third-party ads and trackers (minimal impact on site functionality)
||doubleclick.net^
||googlesyndication.com^
||adservice.*^
||track.*^
||analytics.*^
||cloudfront.net^
||googletagservices.com^
||amazon-adsystem.com^
||appnexus.com^
||rubiconproject.com^
||facebook.com^$third-party
||twitter.com^$third-party
||instagram.com^$third-party
||youtube.com^$third-party
||vimeo.com^$third-party

! Block video ads and unwanted autoplay (minimized impact)
||*.video-ad.*
||youtube.com/get_video_info?adformat^
||youtube.com/api/stats/ads^
||*.autoplay.*

! Block pop-up ads, banners, and interstitials (soft blocking)
##.popup
##.cookie-banner
##[class^="ad-"]
##.fullscreen-overlay

! Block auto-opening tabs and forced redirects (without breaking site navigation)
||*.redirect.*$document
||*.offer.*$document
||*.promo.*$document
||*.ad.*$document
||*.track.*$document
||*.landing.*$document

! Block malware, phishing, and suspicious links (essential for mobile)
||*.malware.*
||*.phishing.*
||*.virus.*
||*.fraud.*
||*.scam.*

! Exceptions for trusted sites to load essential resources (keeps websites functional)
@@||trustedwebsite.com^$document
@@||example.com^$document
@@||fonts.gstatic.com^$font
@@||googleapis.com^$script
@@||cdnjs.cloudflare.com^$script
@@||*.google.com^$script
@@||*.cloudflare.com^$script
