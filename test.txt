! Auto-Opening Tab Blocker - Blocks Auto-Opening Tabs Without Breaking Websites
! This filter is designed to prevent unwanted popups and redirects, ensuring site functionality.

! Block auto-opening tabs, forced redirects, and unwanted windows (prevents new tab popups)
||*://*.click.*^$document
||*://*.offer.*^$document
||*://*.open.*^$document
||*://*.newwindow.*^$document
||*://*.redirect.*^$document
||*://*.popup.*^$document
||*://*.auto-redirect.*^$document

! End of List
